About Rawc-On!

Rawc-On! is an advanced music experience platform that leverages the Spotify API to provide personalized, data-driven insights and recommendations. Our service enhances your music journey by offering:

  • Detailed statistical profiles of your top tracks, artists, and genres, across different time periods (4 weeks, 6 months, all time).
  • Personalized music recommendations based on user-defined seeds and desired audio characteristics.
  • Advanced playlist management features and statistics, including:
    • Custom playlist cover image generation.
    • Duplicate track removal.
    • Intelligent track reordering.
    • Tailored track recommendations for playlist enhancement.

How It Works

Rawc-On! securely connects to your Spotify account using Spotify's OAuth 2.0 flow. Upon authorization, we analyze your listening history and preferences to generate insights and power our recommendation engine. The platform uses Spotify's Web API to fetch detailed information about your playlists, tracks, and artists, which is then processed to provide you with a customized music experience.

Data Usage and Privacy

Rawc-On! is committed to protecting your privacy and adheres to modern web security standards. We collect and process data from your Spotify account to provide our services. This includes, but is not limited to, your listening history, playlists, and favorite artists. The data is used solely to enhance your experience on Rawc-On! and is not shared with third parties.

  • Data Collected: We access your Spotify user ID, the country your account is registered to, and lists of your top artists & tracks. This data is used to generate your profile, visualizations, and power the recommendation engine.
  • Security: Rawc-On! uses secure authentication processes managed by Spotify, ensuring that your login is as secure as logging into Spotify itself.
  • Data Retention: We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services. You can request data deletion at any time by contacting us.
